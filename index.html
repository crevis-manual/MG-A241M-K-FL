1. Product Overview

The MG-A241M/K-FL represents the pinnacle of industrial imaging, featuring the renowned Sony¬Æ Pregius S IMX540 global shutter sensor. By delivering a staggering 24.5 megapixel resolution through an one Gigabit Ethernet interface, it provides the exceptional clarity and precision required for advanced semiconductor inspection, flat-panel display quality control, and large-scale battery manufacturing.

2. Key Features

Sony Pregius S Technology Utilizes the 4th generation IMX540 sensor for back-illuminated pixel structures, ensuring higher sensitivity and lower noise.
GigE Vision Standard Fully compliant with GigE Vision 1.2 and GenICam standards, allowing for seamless integration with universal software libraries.
Advanced PoE Capability Simplifies deployment with Power over Ethernet, reducing cabling complexity while maintaining high data reliability.
Compact Industrial Design A rugged 29x29x40mm housing ensures the camera can be integrated into space-critical machinery without sacrificing thermal performance.
