High Resolution GigE Vision Camera

MG-A241M/K-FL

Sony CMOS Pregius S IMX540 | 24.5 MP | GigE Interface

1. Product Overview

The MG-A241 series is an ultra-high resolution industrial camera equipped with the Sony® Pregius S IMX540 global shutter sensor. Combining 24.5 MP resolution with a GigE interface, it provides the optimal inspection environment for semiconductor precision inspection, display, and secondary battery manufacturing processes.

2. Key Features

Sony Pregius S Sensor The high-performance IMX540 global shutter sensor captures fast-moving subjects clearly without distortion.
GigE Vision Standard Supports the standard GigE Vision interface, allowing long-distance data transmission up to 100m without repeaters.
PoE (Power over Ethernet) Supports PoE functionality, enabling simplified wiring and reduced installation costs by transmitting power and data over a single cable.
Compact Housing Designed with a compact size of 29 x 29 x 40 mm, making it easy to install inside equipment with tight space constraints.
